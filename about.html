Posts Bio Resume

I'm a software engineer in Los Angeles working on platform engineering at PwC. I build infrastructure for payment systems, tokenization platforms, and cloud-native applications using Go, Python, TypeScript, Kubernetes, and Terraform. Before PwC, I spent seven years at Cubic Transportation Systems shipping fare collection systems for DC Metro, NYC MTA, BART, and LA Metro.

Outside of work, I founded The Archive of American Radio, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit preserving American radio drama. We built Anthology, a mobile app for listening to public-domain audio from the golden age of radio. I also built Timer Fantasy, a macOS menu bar app with beautiful analog timers and iCloud sync, and Io Pictures Corporation, a film distribution company that has sold rights to Tubi, Amazon, Apple, and Google.

I write about Python on Dev.to and share open-source tools on GitHub. I studied linguistics and journalism at Penn State, then got a master's in industrial engineering from UT Tyler. I'm a U.S. citizen based in Los Angeles.
