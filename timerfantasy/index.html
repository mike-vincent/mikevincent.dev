Timer Fantasy

Beautiful analog timers for your Mac. Set timers with a simple drag on an elegant clock face, and watch time count down in a way that's intuitive and satisfying.

 

Getting started

Timer Fantasy is available on the Mac App Store. Download it to start using beautiful analog timers on your Mac.

Once installed, click the + button to create your first timer. Drag on the clock face to set the duration, or use the preset buttons for quick access to common times like 5 minutes, 15 minutes, or 1 hour.

Requirements

  • Mac running macOS 14 Sonoma or later
  • iCloud account for syncing across devices (optional)
 

Features

Multiple Clock Face Scales

Choose from 16 different clock faces ranging from 5 minutes to 96 hours. The clock face automatically adapts to show time in a way that makes sense—a 5-minute timer shows a 5-minute scale, while an 8-hour timer shows hours.

Auto Zoom

Let the app automatically choose the best clock face for your timer. As time counts down, it can zoom in to show more detail when you need it most.

Multiple Timers

Create as many timers as you need. Each timer runs independently with its own settings, colors, and alarm sounds. Perfect for cooking, workouts, or managing multiple tasks.

Customization

  • 15 built-in macOS alarm sounds
  • Adjustable alarm duration from 1 to 60 seconds
  • Custom timer colors or automatic color assignment
  • Looping timers that repeat automatically
  • Editable timer labels for easy identification

iCloud Sync

Your timers sync automatically across all your Macs using iCloud. Start a timer on one Mac and see it on another.

 

Your Privacy

Timer Fantasy does not collect any personal information. The app runs entirely on your device. If you use iCloud sync, your timer data is stored in your personal iCloud account—we never have access to it. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising.
