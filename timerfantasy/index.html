Timer Fantasy

Beautiful analog timers for your Mac. Set timers with a simple drag on an elegant clock face, and watch time count down in a way that's intuitive and satisfying.

 

Getting started

Timer Fantasy is available on the Mac App Store. Download it to start using beautiful analog timers on your Mac.

Once installed, click the + button to create your first timer. Drag on the clock face to set the duration, or use the preset buttons for quick access to common times like 5 minutes, 15 minutes, or 1 hour.

Requirements

  • Mac running macOS 14 Sonoma or later
  • iCloud account for syncing across devices (optional)
 

Clock face scales

Choose from 16 different clock faces ranging from 5 minutes to 96 hours. The clock face automatically adapts to show time in a way that makes sense—a 5-minute timer shows a 5-minute scale, while an 8-hour timer shows hours.

Available scales

  • Short timers: 5m, 9m, 15m, 30m
  • Hour timers: 60m, 90m, 120m
  • Long timers: 4h, 8h, 12h, 16h, 24h
  • Multi-day timers: 48h, 72h, 96h

Auto Zoom

Let the app automatically choose the best clock face for your timer. As time counts down, it can zoom in to show more detail when you need it most. You can also manually cycle through clock faces by tapping the scale button.

 

Multiple timers

Create as many timers as you need. Each timer runs independently with its own settings, colors, and alarm sounds. Perfect for cooking, workouts, or managing multiple tasks at once.

Click the + button to add a new timer. Each timer can have its own label, making it easy to identify what each timer is tracking. Timers are displayed in a grid that automatically adjusts to fit your window size.

Managing timers

To start a timer, drag on the clock face to set the time, then tap the play button. While running, you can pause, resume, or cancel the timer at any time. When a timer completes, it will play your chosen alarm sound.

To delete a timer, tap the trash icon when the timer is idle. A confirmation dialog will appear to prevent accidental deletion.

 

Customization

Timer Fantasy offers extensive customization options to make your timers work exactly how you want them.

Alarm sounds

Choose from 15 built-in macOS alarm sounds including Basso, Blow, Bottle, Frog, Funk, Glass, Hero, Morse, Ping, Pop, Purr, Sosumi, Submarine, Tink, and the default system sound. You can also adjust the alarm duration from 1 to 60 seconds.

Colors

Each timer can have its own color, making it easy to distinguish between multiple timers at a glance. You can choose a specific color or enable auto-color to have the app automatically assign colors to new timers.

Looping

Enable looping to have a timer automatically restart when it completes. This is perfect for interval training, pomodoro technique, or any situation where you need repeating timers.

Labels

Give each timer a custom label to identify what it's tracking. Tap the timer name to edit it. Labels sync across all your devices via iCloud.

 

iCloud sync

Your timers sync automatically across all your Macs using iCloud. Create a timer on one Mac and see it appear on another. Timer settings, labels, colors, and preferences all stay in sync.

iCloud sync is optional and requires an iCloud account. If you don't use iCloud, your timers will be stored locally on each device.

 

Your Privacy and Data

Timer Fantasy does not collect any personal information. The app runs entirely on your device with no analytics, tracking, or advertising. If you use iCloud sync, your timer data is stored in your personal iCloud account—we never have access to it. For more information, visit our Privacy Policy.
